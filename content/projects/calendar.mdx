A subscription tracker that lays every recurring payment on a monthly calendar, so renewal dates and total spend stay visible at a glance.

Color-coded indicators distinguish monthly cycles in cyan from yearly cycles in green, with auto-calculated cost summaries for the month. Add, edit, or delete subscriptions through a quick dialog, and logos load automatically from each service's domain.

Built with Next.js 16 + React 19 and styled using Tailwind CSS 4 with a neubrutalism aesthetic. Local-storage persistence keeps everything client-side, no backend required.
